# Logo & Branding - Prompt-Patterns

> Starke Kategorie fuer Markenlogos, Monogramme, Wachssiegel-Optik und 3D-Logo-Renderings. Nano Banana Pro erzeugt praezise Typografie und flache Vektorgrafik-Aesthetik.

## Empfohlenes Format

JSON-Objekte mit klarer Trennung von Hauptelement, Sekundaerelement, Farbpalette und Finish-Vorgaben. Alternativ strukturierter Fliesstext mit Abschnitten fuer jedes Designelement.

## Best Practice Patterns

### Pattern 1: Elegantes Script-Monogramm mit Markenlogo

Erzeugt stilisierte Einzelbuchstaben-Monogramme im ornamentalen Vektorstil mit dem offiziellen Markenlogo darunter. Perfekt fuer luxurioese Markenpraesentationen.

```
A flat, minimalist vector design featuring a highly stylized single letter monogram with the official brand logo below it, representing [BRAND NAME].

**MAIN ELEMENT (THE MONOGRAM):**
The center of the design features ONLY the single first letter of "[BRAND NAME]". The style is "Ornamental Vector Typography" with high calligraphic artistry, mimicking bespoke luxury monograms. This is not a standard font; it is a Highly customized, artistic glyph with elegant flourishes, exaggerated swashes, or ornamental serifs. The lines are clean, precise, and perfectly smooth vector graphics.

**SECONDARY ELEMENT (BRAND IDENTIFIER):**
Directly below the main stylized letter, positioned centrally, include the actual, original official logomark or logotype of "[BRAND NAME]". This element must be rendered significantly smaller than the main letter above it.

**FINISH & TEXTURE (STRICT):**
Absolutely zero texture, zero embossing, zero shadows, and zero gradients anywhere in the image. It must look like a perfectly flat digital illustration or screen print.

**COLOR PALETTE (AI-DRIVEN & HIGH CONTRAST):**
1.  **Background:** A single, solid, flat color block chosen by the AI to represent the vibe of [BRAND NAME].
2.  **Main Monogram Color:** A single, solid, flat color that creates a sharp, high contrast against the background.
3.  **Secondary Logo Color:** The small official logo below must be monochrome. It must be EITHER solid white OR solid dark gray--chosen automatically based on whichever offers the best readability against the chosen background color.
```

### Pattern 2: Vintage-Kamera-Suchseite mit kreativem Branding

Kreative Neuinterpretation bekannter Web-Interfaces mit Marken-Elementen. Minimaler Prompt mit maximaler visueller Wirkung.

```
A screenshot of the Google home page with a banana Google doodle, change I'm feeling lucky to be "Go bananas". No monkeys.
```

### Pattern 3: Gigantisches Alltagsobjekt als Monument

Verwandelt alltaegliche Objekte in fotorealistische Monumente mit Markenwirkung. Ideal fuer kreatives Branding und Kampagnenvisualisierung.

```
Transform [EVERYDAY OBJECT] into a massive real-world monument. Surface materials are physically accurate, with visible wear, scratches, dust, and scale references like people and vehicles. Shot from a low-angle cinematic perspective, realistic daylight, ultra-detailed textures.
```

## Kategoriespezifische Tipps

- **Flache Vektorgrafik**: Explizit "zero texture, zero embossing, zero shadows, zero gradients" angeben
- **Farbpalette**: AI-gesteuerte Farbwahl mit "high contrast" funktioniert hervorragend
- **Monogramme**: "Ornamental Vector Typography" und "calligraphic artistry" als Stilbegriffe verwenden
- **Markentreue**: "original official logomark or logotype" sichert korrekte Logo-Darstellung
- **Skalierung**: Groessenverhaeltnis zwischen Hauptelement und Sekundaerelement klar definieren

## Empfohlene Negative Prompts

```
texture, embossing, shadows, gradients, 3D effects, photorealistic rendering, blurry edges, low resolution, watermark, multiple fonts, busy background, cluttered composition
```
